Some notes

Astrophotography is one of the most challenging types of photography, as it requires specialized equipment, precise planning, and a healthy dose of patience in oftentimes difficult conditions. Nevertheless, the rewards can be truly breathtaking, as capturing the beauty of the universe above us is an experience that few can match. I hope through this selection of my favorite space photos, you can appreciate the wonder and majesty of the cosmos as much as it deserves.
