About :

Welcome to my website. Here I showcase some of my favorite photos I've taken over the years, whether they're aviation, nature or other. I'm a student at the Gymnase du Bugnon, Switzerland, in the 3rd year. I started photography in 2023, and haven't stopped ever since. This website is a project for school, although I have taken great pleasure in its creation. My favorite genre is Aviation, but I also enjoy playing with light and perspective, with everyday objects and stunning views, in varied situations. Another thing I like about this hobby is that no two photos are ever the same, and adaptability is key.
