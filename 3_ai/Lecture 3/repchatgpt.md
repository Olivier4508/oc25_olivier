1. Astronomy (surface gravity from spectral lines)

Correct answer: A) El2 I (neutral)

Explanation:

Surface gravity affects electron pressure in the stellar atmosphere. Spectral lines that are from the minority ionization stage are the most sensitive to changes in gravity.

El1 → mostly neutral, so El1 I is the majority species (less sensitive).

El2 → mostly ionized, so El2 I is the minority species (very sensitive to pressure/gravity).

Therefore, the lines most sensitive to surface gravity are El2 I.

2. Organic Chemistry (fulvene + ethyl acrylate reaction)

Correct answer: C) 8

Explanation:

The fulvene derivative behaves as a diene.

Ethyl acrylate acts as a dienophile.

The reaction is a Diels–Alder cycloaddition, which removes the cross-conjugation (hence the yellow color disappears).

Because the fulvene precursor exists as several positional isomers, the cycloaddition can produce multiple regioisomeric products. Counting the possible constitutional arrangements (ignoring stereochemistry) gives 8 chemically distinct isomers.

3. Molecular Biology (protein not synthesized)

Correct answer: B) Trimethylation of lysine of H3 histone in position 27 at the promoter of the gene encoding the target protein

Explanation:

H3K27 trimethylation (H3K27me3) is a well-known epigenetic repression mark.

When present at a gene promoter, it suppresses transcription, meaning the mRNA is not produced, so the protein is not synthesized.
